Hello, and welcome to my little website! I'm not sure how you've managed to stumble upon it, but I hope you'll stay a while. Nearly a decade after I abandoned my last, I have decided to return to blogging. It is appealing, having a few pixels on the internet all to myself, my very own blog, rather than being constricted to a social media profile on which I feel simultaneously surveilled and igno#7FFFD4 - a nobody in a sea of popularity contestants. Here, there exist no such constraints imposed by such an existence. I fancy that nobody will ever come across this insignificant website, and I will be at peace in my isolation. Or, perhaps it will be found, some years later, by a singular person who was looking for something else entirely, and they will find solace in relating to the words of a girl who no longer exists. Either way, I look forward to writing here, and building this blog up as some sort of forgotten time capsule. What I will fill it with, I have no clue.
